I want to be much better at sharing critical feedback in professional contexts. Right now I feel afraid and nervous when I imagine sharing critical feedback; I’d like instead to feel calm and steady.

I still do share critical feedback relatively often, perhaps much more often than other people,[^1] but still not enough to be really effective.

Having critical feedback but holding it in is such a waste—of team productivity, of strengthening bonds with teammates, of my own emotional state. It’s often clearly the right long-term decision to share feedback (and it’s notable to me that many of the people who I admire professionally are very good at sharing critical feedback with equanimity) yet it’s very hard to do so in any particular case.
